Kylie (Ky) Sharron is an aspiring clinical researcher and psychologist. Ky is currently working in behavioral healthcare administration at The Motherhood Center of New York while conducting psychiatric research focused primarily on suicide and applying to PhD programs in clinical psychology. Ky's goals in both research and clinical work are to work with high-acuity populations in high-risk settings to reduce self-inflicted harm in vulnerable communities.
